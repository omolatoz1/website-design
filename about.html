About Us


The ClassiqueMO Way refers to our organizational culture. It reflects our commitment to safety, world class quality service delivery, innovation, empathy and customer satisfaction.

Our culture is driven by our vision, our mission, and our core values, which when put together act as a compass to guide us on the path of excellence.

Our Vision


To be a world class Integrated Energy Services company.

Our Mission


To promote a culture of excellence through commitment, innovation, reliability, safety and cost-effectiveness. Our Values Professional excellence, Integrity and Honesty in all business dealings
with our stakeholders, clients and customers.
